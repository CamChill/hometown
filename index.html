Visit Tempe

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Arizona.

Top three activities to do in Tempe

Tempe Town Lake

The lake located in downtown Tempe offers boating, paddleboarding, fishing, and music festivals.

Gammage Auditorium

The auditorium located at Arizona State University hosts local theatre and touring performers.

Tempe Center for the Arts

Overlooking Tempe Town Lake, the center has a 3,500 square-foot gallery dedicated to the arts.
